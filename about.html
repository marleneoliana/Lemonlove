The first winery that specialize in making wine lemon and beverages made with lemon wine. We are trying to reach that goal by making wine with Lemon, a shocking combination of word that turns delicious. Our winery starts off small from an experiment in a small house and slowly our bussines has grown. Now we have a whole garden full of lemons and 2 storage unit to store and ferment our lemons ourselves, not to mention now we have a small store to sell beverages and wine.
